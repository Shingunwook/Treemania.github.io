About us

Treemania is a non-profit project whose overall goal is to improve the environment in the world, increase people's understanding of how to treat nature, plant new trees and improve people's overall health. thanks to its professional approach and innovative solutions, it has become a popular choice for those who appreciate beauty and nature

Treemania has many years of experience, several regular clients in the form of large firms, a large number of completed projects of varying degrees of importance and complexity
